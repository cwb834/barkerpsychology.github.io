About Our Lab

The Barker Psychology Lab @ St. Francis Xavier University utilizes qualitative research methods to explore topics in Educational Psychology.

Ongoing projects in the institute include the Adverse Educational Experiences study, as well as the development and evaluation of neuroscience knowledge translation activities
