Prospective Researchers

The Barker Psychology lab conducts it's research during the summer semester, and is always on the look out for new talent to join us for the next research session. Should you be interested, please email Dr. Barker at conor.barker1@msvu.ca for potential opportunities!.

Prospective Graduate Students

Dr. Barker is available to supervise potential Master's or Doctoral candidates. Should you be interested in discussing options, please email Dr. Barker at conor.barker1@msvu.ca to set up an initial consultation.
